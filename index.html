Purplex Marketing

Internal Content Manager · September 2024–Present

Joined Purplex as a Content Writer and was promoted to Internal Content Manager within 13 months, bypassing standard progression to work directly within The Ascot Group. The role expanded into content direction, business strategy, and campaign planning across Purplex Marketing, Insight Data, and the personal brands of senior leadership.

Content output spanned video scripts, long-form articles, web pages, press releases, thought-leadership columns, email campaigns, and social media. Most work was for clients in construction, fenestration, and manufacturing.

Key work

  • Contributed to a national award-winning PR campaign — Warwick North West winning Fabricator of the Year
  • Wrote and structured email marketing campaigns with defined sales funnels, balancing educational content with sales emails and bespoke landing pages that drove lead generation
  • Produced SEO content, PR copy, web pages, and social posts across multiple client accounts
  • Contributed to brand and content strategy at senior level within The Ascot Group

Taunton Town F.C.

Assistant Head of Media · July 2023–2025

Started at Taunton Town as a live matchday reporter, writing real-time social updates from the ground. Within the first year, the role expanded into filming and editing. By the end, I was operating as Assistant Head of Media and lead videographer across all club output.

Multi-camera match coverage, YouTube highlight packages, training footage, player interviews, and graphics all became part of my output. The focus was always on content that connected with fans and reflected the club's identity.

Key work

  • Grew from matchday reporter to assistant head and lead videographer
  • Filmed and edited multi-camera match coverage and highlight packages for YouTube and social media
  • Contributed to the club's digital strategy, producing content that reached beyond the existing fanbase
  • Managed writing, social media, video production, and technical filming across a full season
